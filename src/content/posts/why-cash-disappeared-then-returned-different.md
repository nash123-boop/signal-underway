For 72 hours, cash behaved like a rumor.

Not banned in a headline. Just friction everywhere.

## The Trace

- Retailers suddenly pushed card-only workflows.
- ATM behavior changed in ways that looked coordinated.
- Messaging was inconsistent, which can be a feature.

## Two Plausible Interpretations

1. **Stress test:** See what breaks when cash stops being reliable.
2. **Behavior shaping:** Measure compliance without announcing the experiment.

## The Question That Matters

If you can switch cash off briefly, what happens when you do it longer with better messaging?
